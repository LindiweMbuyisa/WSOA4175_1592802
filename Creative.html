Creative Projects

This section is all for the creative projects

Car Crash

Car Crash is a 2D single player game. The player controls a car which is on the wrong side of the road
The goal of the game is to avoid the incoming cars. In the game there is powerups that can make the car invisible to the incoming cars, powerup that make the car small and retract the car back to its original size.

Illusion

Illusion is a 2D picture sliding block puzzle. The player has to rearrange the blocks by selcting the block to move it up,down, left or right. This game which a recreation of the classic puzzle sliding block puzzle game, it takes a different approach in terms of the rtoation puzzle blocks and the pictures in the game which play around illusion.

Find

This game is about finding the right path to ensure each of the block characters reach their goal however the player is in control of both the characters.
This game features inverse mechanic- whereby the block characters move in opposite direction. So while one of the characters moves up, the other move down.
