Car Crash

Car Crash is a 2D single player game. The player controls a car which is on the wrong side of the road. The goal of the game is to avoid the incoming cars. In the game there is powerups that can make the car invisible making it easier to dodge the cars, powerups that make the car small and retract the car back to its original size.

Role: Designer, Programmer and Interface designer
